The story of Mayday is pre-written and set in stone. However, every historical event has many sides, and humans are very complex, nuanced, and even paradoxical beings. Thus this story is best told and read through many perspectives.

Unfortunately, minor damages to the system prevent me from uploading several documents at a time. It appears I am limited to sending you only one document every week. It is unfortunate, but it is the only way. As a compromise, I am willing to let you all collectively vote for a crew member whose perspective you wish to view every week.


RULES:

  1. Every chapter, save for the prologue, follows one of the 12 crew members on board the A-2020. The next chapter's point character is dictated by majority vote through the poll at the end of each chapter (there is no other method.)
  2. After winning, a crew member cannot be voted for in the next round. This means no crew member can win twice in a row.
  3. If a crew member is asleep, unconscious, or deceased by the time the next chapter starts, they are ineligible to win.
  4. Ineligible crew members won't always immediately be removed as a poll option.
  5. If two or more crew members tie for the vote, one will be chosen at random.
  6. Poll statistics will only be revealed once The A-2020 has reached its destination.
