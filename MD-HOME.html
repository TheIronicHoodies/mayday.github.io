Earth is no more. We have killed it.


Greetings, end users.

The last 12 humans have managed to live in mutual prosperity aboard the ARK-2020 (often abbreviated to 'The A-2020' or just 'The 2020'), an interstellar voyager en route to the nearest habitable exoplanet. Sadly, 22 years ago, it crashed.

While several recording devices were present onboard, I am unable to analyze and understand all of the data by myself. My goal is to understand humans. I suspect that the cause of their ultimate demise was the build-up of unresolved conflicts and inner turmoil.

Unfortunately, this system can only handle so much. It appears I am limited to sending you only one document every week. It is unfortunate, but it is the only way. As a compromise, I am willing to let you all collectively vote for a crew member whose perspective you wish to view every week.


-——<<-RULES->>——-
1. Every chapter after the prologue follows one of the A-2020's 12 crew personnel. The next chapter's point character is dictated by majority vote through the poll found in the latest chapter.
-——<<-RULES->>——-
2. After winning, a crew member cannot be voted for in the next round. This means no crew member can win twice in a row.
-——<<-RULES->>——-
3. If a crew member is asleep, unconscious, or deceased by the time the next chapter starts, they are ineligible to win.
-——<<-RULES->>——-
4. If two or more crew members are tied, one will be chosen at random.
-——<<-RULES->>——-
5. Poll statistics will only be revealed once the human race has become extinct.
-——<<-RULES->>——-
6. Additional rules may be implemented when deemed necessary.

I trust that you will all be nice to each other when making a decision on who to vote for, no matter who or where you are and where you choose to communicate. Not everyone may be satisfied with the winning vote, and that's okay.
