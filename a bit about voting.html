Hi folks! Here's a tad more info about the voting process:

Even among people I know, everyone's votes are kept completely anonymous, and the poll at the end of a chapter is the only people can get votes in to ensure fairness. I'll let y'all flatter me in my email and DMs, but it's not going to change anything. ;3

But what you CAN do if you want a higher chance to see your choice win is to gather a couple of friends and convince them: Why should we all vote for X? These days, we all have Facebook, Twitter, Discord, touching grass, and many more avenues of communication.

As of now, I'm using Strawpoll as a means for people to vote, but once it's done I'll be using a built-in poll instead. It'll take a while, but I'll let you know when that's up and ready!

Voting closes within 30 minutes before the next chapter is released to give ample time to crunch the numbers and update the site accordingly.

That's all for now, folks! Ciao,
Yar Boi Levi
